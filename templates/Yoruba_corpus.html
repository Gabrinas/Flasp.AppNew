To fully utilise this recording and get the expected recording, kindly take note of the following instructions:

1. Select one sentence at a time from the transcript page.
2. Ensure your accent is properly taking care of in the recording.
3. Ensure you are cautious of the pace at which you pronounce each word in the sentence.
Do not be too fast and do not be too slow.
4. Ensure your recorded words match that provided in the transcript index chosen. If you mistakenly
deviate, kindly reload the page to start over.
5. Save the file with index no (in the transcript)_YC.wav starting with index 000001 e.g.
000001_YC.wav
6. Upload your recordings to google drive using the link https://www.... while simultaneously submit
the details of your recordings using the form on the left hand side of the screen.
7.Record a maximum of 2.5 hours and take a break every 30 minutes of recordings.

Also submit the following details per audio recording:
1. File name you save the recording with.
2. The type of data you just recorded (either general or public health)
3. Duration (in seconds) of each of your recordings
4. Accent type utilised for the recording.
