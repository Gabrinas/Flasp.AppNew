You are welcome to this page

This web page has developed to collect parallel accented English-Yoruba speech corporal for speech synthesis research work. The aim is to obtain naturally accented corporal of both English and Yoruba, and these corporal is to be made available for public use for research related to speech synthsis.
To participate in this activities, simply register to login in your details. Create a user name and choose a password.

Kindly note:
(i) The text or transcripts to be recorded are available in the Transcript Page
(ii) To participate in the recording of the Yoruba speech corpus, click here Yoruba Corpus Page or navigate using the above the link.
(iii) To participate in the recording of the English speech corpus, click here English Corpus Page or navigate using the above the link.

    Speech Synthesis

  • This is the generation, creation or syhthesis of speech in another language.
  • In this project, English and Yoruba are the source and target languages respectively.
  • The main focus of this work is to develop English-Yoruba parallel corporal for speech synthsis of this project.
  • To assist in the future reasearch work in speech processing, recognition, and synthesis, the coproral to be generated in this project would be publicly made available for reserach purposes.
